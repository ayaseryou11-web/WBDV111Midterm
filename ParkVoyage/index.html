About Pradera Islands Theme Park

Our Vision

Pradera Islands Theme Park envisions becoming a premier 23-hectare "Pinoy-themed" destination that blends world-class attractions with Filipino culture—creating a vibrant hub for tourism, adventure, and unforgettable experiences in Central Luzon.

Our Story

Built within the Pradera Verde complex, Park Voyage was designed to elevate regional tourism by combining thrill rides, water attractions, and cultural storytelling. It complements existing facilities such as the golf course, wakeboard park, and flying club—creating a complete leisure destination.
